Our Story

From Garage to Global Repair Leader

Founded in 2010, RepairPro started with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable, and high-quality repair services for everyday electronics. What began as a one-man operation in a small garage has now grown into a full-service repair center with a team of certified experts.

We believe in extending the lifespan of your devices to reduce e-waste and save you money. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of thousands of satisfied customers.

Our Mission

To extend the lifespan of electronic devices, reducing e-waste and providing cost-effective solutions for our community. We strive to be the most trusted name in repair by prioritizing honesty, quality, and speed.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of tech support and repair services globally, recognized for our innovation, sustainability practices, and exceptional customer service standards.
