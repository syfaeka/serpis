5 Signs Your Battery Needs Replacement

Is your smartphone struggling to make it through the day? Battery degradation is unavoidable, but knowing the signs can save you from unexpected shutdowns.

Batteries are consumable components that become less effective as they chemically age. Here are the top 5 signs it's time for a swap:

1. Rapid Draining

If you notice your battery percentage dropping significantly even when you're not using the phone heavily, it's a clear sign of capacity loss.

2. Unexpected Shutdowns

Does your phone turn off when it still shows 20% or 30% battery? This happens when the battery can no longer deliver the peak power required by the processor.

"Replacing your battery is the most cost-effective way to extend your device's lifespan by another 1-2 years."

3. Overheating

A degrading battery generates more heat during charging and discharging. If your phone feels hot to the touch during normal use, be careful.
